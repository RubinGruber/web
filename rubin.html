Rubin Gruber

About Me

Rubin Gruber is a visionary entrepreneur with over 40 years of groundbreaking experience in telecommunications and digital networking. His career spans from pioneering packet-switching technologies at BBN Communications to founding Sonus Networks in 1997—revolutionizing voice-over-IP.

He further advanced digital content delivery with the founding of Verivue, Inc. in 2006, and now serves as Chairman of Metrika, Inc. His innovative leadership continues to drive transformative technologies in blockchain analytics and decentralized networks.

Committed to giving back, Rubin actively mentors emerging talent and supports educational initiatives, including the Rubin and Cindy Gruber Sandbox at Florida Atlantic University.
